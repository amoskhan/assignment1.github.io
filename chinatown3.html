Stop The Mega Jail: Chinatown Needs Your Help

Chinatown still has not fully recovered from September 11th, the closure of Park Row, ongoing construction of Brooklyn Bridge, and COVID-19. Chinatown cannot become a construction site and home of the worlds largest jail.

The proposed jail will use both Centre Street and Baxter Streets for construction, an already densely populated area with senior housing, green space, and small businesses.

Contact Mayor Adams to stop the construction. Chinatown needs your help.

Launching Our New Longevity Fund Accelerator with Too Good To Go

We're excited to announce that Welcome to Chinatown is launching a new Longevity Fund Accelerator in partnership with Too Good To Go, an anti-food waste app! Our goal is to reduce food waste and develop additional revenue streams for Chinatown food establishments.
